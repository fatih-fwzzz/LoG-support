Privacy Policy

Legend of Gardatara

Effective Date: August 31, 2025

Thank you for playing The Legend of Gardatara. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our game. Our philosophy is simple: we want you to enjoy the game without worrying about your personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We designed The Legend of Gardatara to be played without collecting your personal information. We do not collect, store, or have access to any personal data that can identify you, such as:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your physical address or location
  • Your photos, contacts, or other data from your device
  • Any information related to payments or billing

The game does not require you to create an account or sign in.

2. Information Collected Automatically (Non-Personal)

To improve your gameplay experience, we may collect anonymous, non-personal data. This information cannot be used to identify you and includes:

  • Gameplay Data: Such as levels completed, heroes used, and game session length. This helps us balance the game and fix bugs.
  • Device Information: Such as your device type (e.g., iPhone 15) and operating system version (e.g., iOS 18). This helps us ensure the game runs smoothly on different devices.

3. Apple Game Center Integration

The Legend of Gardatara uses Apple's Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. If you choose to sign in to Game Center, your Game Center username and scores will be visible to other Game Center users.

This service is operated by Apple. We do not have access to your Apple ID or any personal information associated with your Game Center account. Your interaction with Game Center is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect any personal identifiable information from children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction). As we do not collect personal information in the first place, we do not have a way of identifying a player's age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
