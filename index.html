Legend of Gardatara is a lane-based strategy game where Indonesian folklore heroes fight an alien invasion. Join the clever mousedeer, Si Kancil, on a mission to save other legends from abduction. Explore familiar landscapes, deploy heroes with unique abilities, and push back the mysterious invaders. Start your adventure with Si Kancil on a heroic quest to save the archipelago's greatest stories!

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I play The Legend of Gardatara?

Tap the hero cards at the bottom of the screen to spawn units on the battlefield. They will automatically attack enemies and the enemy tower. Manage your "Magic Book" resource to overwhelm your opponent!

2. Is The Legend of Gardatara based on a true story?

No, the game's story is fully fictional, but all the heroes (like Si Kancil) are inspired by beloved characters from Indonesian folklore and mythology.

3. How can I get more heroes?

Unfortunately currently this game only playable with one hero, but will be available in the future!

4. What platforms is The Legend of Gardatara available on?

The game is currently available on the App Store only for iPhone

5. Is this game suitable for all ages?

The Legend of Gardatara features mild cartoon action. It is generally suitable for all ages, but we recommend it for ages 13+
